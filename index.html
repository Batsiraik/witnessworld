Five ways to connect on WWC

Each module has a clear purpose — so the right listings reach the right people. Browse below to learn what you can find and post in each area.

Local exchange

Marketplace

For neighbors buying, selling, or giving away household items locally — not shippable retail inventory.

Give quality items a second home. Decluttering? Moving? Offering something for free? This is where brothers and sisters trade household goods face-to-face in their local area. Items that need shipping belong in the Online Store instead.

When posting, you can mark an item as FREE under price — perfect for giveaways and acts of kindness.

What you can find

Used furniture, appliances, electronics, clothing, baby gear, garden tools, and more from publishers near you.

What you can post

Household items you want to sell, trade, or give away locally. Keep it pick-up friendly — no mail-order inventory here.

Categories

Furniture Appliances Home decor Electronics Clothing & accessories Baby & kids Garden & outdoor Home improvement Sports & fitness Books, media & hobbies Toys & games Pet supplies Office supplies Health & beauty + Other (custom)
Digital & virtual

Professional services

A global hub for expert-level, task-based digital and virtual work.

Showcase your expertise to entrepreneurs and businesses in the truth. Whether you are a creative, a technical specialist, or a strategic consultant, this module helps you find clients and manage professional projects — often fully remote.

What you can find

Skilled brothers and sisters offering design, development, marketing, writing, consulting, and more.

What you can post

Your professional service — scoped by project or hourly rate — for digital and virtual delivery worldwide.

Categories

Graphic design & branding Web & tech development Digital marketing Writing & translation Virtual assistance Business consulting Financial & legal Multimedia + Other (custom)
Retail & brand

Online store

For established brands and entrepreneurs selling consistent, shippable inventory.

Launch a permanent retail presence inside WWC. Built for makers, retailers, and product lines that ship nationwide or worldwide — from handmade goods to professional beauty and wellness brands.

What you can find

New products from verified storefronts — beauty, fashion, artisan goods, specialty foods, ministry supplies, and more.

What you can post

A dedicated storefront with your catalog of new, shippable products — not one-off used household items.

Store categories

Beauty & personal care Health & wellness Fashion & apparel Artisan & handmade Specialty foods Tech & gadgets JW products + Other (custom)
Local business

Business directory

Help the community find and support local physical businesses owned by brothers and sisters.

List your brick-and-mortar or local service business so neighbors can discover you. Add your location, hours, and contact details — ideal for shops, offices, and establishments you visit in person.

What you can find

Trusted local businesses — dining, medical, legal, retail, home services, and professional offices near you.

What you can post

Your business profile with address, category, and how the community can reach you.

Categories

Educational Legal Food & dining Health & medical Home improvement & repair Beauty & spas Professional offices Retail shops + Other (custom)
Community & personal

Community classifieds

Personal needs, community notices, and non-business requests — keeping professional modules focused.

The go-to spot for everyday community life. Looking for a reliable sitter, a roommate, tutoring, or sharing a neighborhood update? Post here to reach brothers and sisters nearby — without mixing personal notices into business listings.

What you can find

Jobs, housing, childcare, pet care, lessons, events, healthcare help, real estate, and local announcements.

What you can post

Personal or community-based notices — not commercial product listings or professional service gigs.

Categories

Careers & jobs Roommates & housing Childcare & babysitting Pet sitting & dog walking Tutoring & lessons Travel & events Healthcare Real estate Vacation homes Transportation Classes + Other (custom)
