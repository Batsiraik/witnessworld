Local exchange

Marketplace

Buy, sell, or give away household items with neighbors — not shippable retail inventory.

Give quality items a second home. Decluttering, moving, or offering something for free? Trade household goods locally. Items that need shipping belong in the Online Store.

Furniture, appliances, electronics, clothing, baby gear, garden tools, and more from publishers near you.
