Request account deletion

You can ask us to permanently delete your Witness World Connect account and the personal data linked to it. This action cannot be undone.

What will be removed: your profile, listings, store products, messages, favorites, and other data tied to your account on our systems.

Already in the app? You can also delete your account under Profile & settings → Delete my account.

This opens your email app with a pre-filled message to support@witnessworldconnect.com. We will verify your identity and process the request, usually within 30 days.
